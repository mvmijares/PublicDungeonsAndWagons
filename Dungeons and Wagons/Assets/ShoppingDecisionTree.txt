NPC Shopping AI 

1. Walk over to shop
2. Stop 
3. Decide to shop ( Show a reaction to decide to shop)
4. Look at each item
5. Show a thinking reaction to each  (Maybe 1-2 seconds)
6. Show a final reaction if they want to buy the item or not.

8. Camera pan over to the NPC to start conversation
